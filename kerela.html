Kerela-God's own country

Kerala, often called "God's Own Country," is a tropical paradise with golden beaches, tranquil backwaters, and lush, green hill stations. Its culture is deeply influenced by its diverse history, blending traditional dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam with vibrant festivals such as Onam. Kerala's rich heritage is reflected in its exquisite art, handloom textiles, and ancient Ayurveda practices.

The state is renowned for its delicious cuisine, particularly its coconut-infused dishes and spicy seafood. The backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom offer serene boat rides through winding canals, surrounded by palm trees and wildlife. Kerala's warm, welcoming people add to the charm of this unique destination.
