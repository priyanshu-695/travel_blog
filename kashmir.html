Kashmir-Heaven on earth

Kashmir, a land of breathtaking beauty, is adorned with snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and serene lakes, creating a paradise on Earth. Its vibrant culture is a harmonious blend of rich traditions, including intricate handwoven carpets, delicate papier-mâché crafts, and famous Pashmina shawls.

The majestic Dal Lake, with its floating houseboats, reflects the serene splendor of this region. The region’s spiritual heritage is deeply rooted in Sufism, with ancient shrines and mosques dotting the landscape.

Kashmiri cuisine, known for its aromatic spices and flavors, is an integral part of the culture. Combining the flavors of past and cultures that left their traces behind.
